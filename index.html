Maintain Healthy Blood Pressure

High blood pressure increases risk for heart and organ disease. Regular check-ups and stress management help keep it in control.

Exercise Regularly

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity weekly. Physical activity strengthens the heart, lungs, and organs.

Eat a Balanced Diet

Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and healthy fats. Plant-based foods support heart, kidneys, and liver health.

Stay Hydrated

Adequate water intake supports kidneys, liver detoxification, and healthy circulation.

Avoid Smoking

Smoking damages blood vessels, lungs, kidneys, and heart. Quitting reduces risks significantly.

Get Quality Sleep

Restful sleep improves heart, brain, and organ function. Aim for 7+ hours every night.
